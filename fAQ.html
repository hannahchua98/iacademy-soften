FAQ

1) I forgot my password. What do I do?
  Enter your email address or contact number and your password will be sent to you.

2) I cancelled my booking. How do I book again?
  Booking can only be triggered once logged in. You must logout and login once again in order to be able
  to book again.

3) I really need to book a seat from a shuttle that is about to depart. What can I do?
  You may ask the guard to force book you as long as your reason for booking is acceptable and necessary.
